## 2) Refund & Cancellation Policy Matrix

## Accommodation (short stays)

### Lenient
- Cancel up to 24h before check-in: **Full refund**
- Cancel within 24h: **50% refund**
- No-show: **No refund**

### Fair (standard)
- Cancel 7+ days before check-in: **Full refund**
- Cancel 2–6 days before check-in: **50% refund**
- Cancel within 48h: **No refund**

### Strict
- Cancel 14+ days before check-in: **50% refund**
- Cancel within 14 days: **No refund**

---

## Monthly stays (30+ days)

### Fair Monthly
- 7–15 days before check-in: **75% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- 3–7 days: **50% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- 0–3 days: **25% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- No-show: **Non-refundable**

### Strict Monthly
- 15–30 days before check-in: **75% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- 7–15 days: **50% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- 0–7 days: **25% refund** (minus service/processing fees)
- No-show: **Non-refundable**

---

## Tours & Experiences

### Standard day tours
- More than 72h before start: **Full refund** (excluding platform fees)
- 48–72h before start: **50% refund** (excluding platform fees)
- Less than 48h before start: **No refund**
- No-show/late arrival: **No refund**

### Multi-day / private / custom
- More than 14 days before start: **Full refund** minus deposits/third-party costs
- 7–14 days before start: **50% refund**
- Less than 7 days before start: **No refund**

---

## Transport / Car Rental
- Cancel 48h+ before pickup: **Full refund**
- Cancel 24–48h before pickup: **50% refund**
- Cancel within 24h: **No refund**
- Early return: **No refund** for unused days

---

## 3) Non-Refundable Components

Typically non-refundable once booked:
- National park/conservation permits
- Gorilla/special access permits
- Third-party accommodation/transport/tickets
- Platform service and payment processing fees (as applicable)
- Any listing item explicitly marked **Non-Refundable**

---

## 4) Refund Processing Times (Typical)

- Mobile Money: **1–3 business days**
- Credit/Debit Cards: **5–10 business days**
- Bank Transfer: **3–7 business days**
- PayPal: **3–5 business days**

---

## 5) Refund Workflow (Support + Product)

1. User opens booking in **My Bookings**
2. User submits cancellation request
3. System evaluates policy window
4. User confirms cancellation + shown refund amount
5. Refund processes back to original payment method

---

## 6) Exception/Dispute Cases

Potential full/partial exceptions outside standard windows:
- Host cancels booking
- Material listing mismatch
- Undisclosed safety hazards
- Natural disasters/government travel restrictions
- Documented medical emergencies
